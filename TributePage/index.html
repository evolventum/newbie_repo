Marilyn Manson

(Brian Hugh Warner)

I never said to be like me, I say be yourself and make a difference.

Here's a time line of Marilyn Manson's life:

  • 1969 - born in Canton, Ohio, USA.
  • 1987 - graduated from GlenOak High School.
  • 1990 - became a student at Broward Community College in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.
  • 1994 - debuted with album "Portrait of an American Family".
  • 1996 - release of the group's second album, "Antichrist Superstar".
  • 1998 - achieved his greatest success with the release of "Mechanical Animals".
  • 1998 - co-wrote his autobiography with noted rock journalist Neil Strauss, titled The Long Road Out of Hell.
  • 1998 - he made his film debut in David Lynch's Lost Highway.
  • 1999 - appear in film Jawbreaker.
  • 2000 - released "Holy Wood (In the Shadow of the Valley of Death)".
  • 2003 - appear in film Party Monster.
  • 2003 - released with band their fifth studio album with "The Golden Age of Grotesque".
  • 2005 - married burlesque dancer Dita Von Teese
  • 2009 - released seventh album, "The High End of Low"
  • 2012 - released album "Born Villain"
  • 2015 - released "The Pale Emperor"
  • 2017 - released 10th album "Heaven Upside Down"
